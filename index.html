Learn English Vocabulary T96

Learn English Vocabulary T96 is a free offline app that helps you master over 3000 of the most common English words through fun quizzes, writing practice, and flashcards. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, this app offers a simple, effective way to grow your vocabulary by CEFR levels – from A1 to C2.

🌟 Why Learn with T96?

Looking for a smart, focused way to learn English vocabulary every day?
This app is perfect for self-learners, students preparing for exams like IELTS or TOEIC, and anyone who wants to improve their English skills without needing Wi-Fi.

🚀 Key Features

  • 📚 3000–5000 English words organized by CEFR levels (A1–C2)
  • 🧠 Level-based quizzes to test and reinforce vocabulary knowledge
  • ✍️ Writing mode: Type the word from definition, with hints and pronunciation
  • 📂 Personal flashcards: Create and manage your own word list
  • ✏️ Edit meanings to fit your learning preferences
  • 📝 Wrong answers saved automatically for review
  • 📕 CEFR Levels:
    • • A1–A2: 1000 words
    • • B1–B2: 3000 words
    • • C1–C2: 5000 words
  • 📴 Offline learning: No internet connection required
  • 🎯 Great for all learners: beginner, intermediate, or advanced

🎯 Perfect for:

  • ✔️ Learners preparing for IELTS, TOEIC, TOEFL
  • ✔️ Beginners starting from A1 level
  • ✔️ Students wanting structured vocabulary practice
  • ✔️ Professionals improving English for work
